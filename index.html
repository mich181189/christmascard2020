Welcome to the Christmas Card 2020 docs!¶

If you are reading this, you have probably received a christmas card from me. Or, maybe, you stumbled upon this project and are curious about what I created. This card is, in fact, a Micropython development board! It can, of course, be used as a simple decoration, but it is also programmable to your liking!
